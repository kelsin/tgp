About

I have worked as a photographer from 1983 until the present. After working at the Hartford Courant as a freelancer in 1983, I worked as an assistant to Jack McConnell, a commercial photographer in Wethersfield, Ct. In 1985 I ventured into the Hartford market as a freelance photographer and have enjoyed servicing clients ever since.

I enjoy a mix of clients from both the corporate world and the world of theatre and dance. My clients include St. Joseph College, Shipman and Goodwin, Pepe & Hazard, Rogin & Nassau, Eastern Connecticut. Ballet, New Haven Ballet, Hartford Children's Theatre, Greater Hartford Academy for the Arts, American Federation of Teachers, and Hartt College Dance Department.

I service a large number of corporate clients here in the New England are, from law firms, insurance companies and financial institutions as well as non-profits. Whether it is an annual report or an in house newsletter, I can come to your location and capture the images which say what you want about your business. Being able to travel to your location and still maintain a high level of lighting and composition saves time and money for your employees and your design team.
