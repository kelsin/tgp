Dance Portraits

Besides photographing rehearsals and performances, I like to photograph dancers in a studio setting. From simple audition photos, in standard poses, to more dramatic movement shots, I can provide an experienced dancer's eye to give you a portfolio that is beneficial to you whether it is for an audition or for a keepsake. The session itself can be educational to you as a dancer. Besides photographing dancers for over 30 years. I teach ballet at several schools in the Hartford area, including the Greater Hartford Academy for the Arts.

Having danced professional for 9 years with the Hartford Ballet and performing many principal roles, my experience helps me to give you images which are technically correct and also artistic and dramatic.

About

I have worked as a photographer from 1983 until the present. After working at the Hartford Courant as a freelancer in 1983, I worked as an assistant to Jack McConnell, a commercial photographer in Wethersfield, Ct. In 1985, I ventured into the Hartford market as a freelance photographer and have enjoyed servicing clients ever since.

I enjoy a mix of clients from both the corporate world and the world of theatre and dance. My clients have included St. Joseph College, Conway Stoughton, Saxe Doernberger & Vita , Eastern Connecticut. Ballet, New Haven Ballet, Ballet Theatre Company, Greater Hartford Academy for the Arts, and Hartt College Dance Department.

I service corporate clients here in the New England area, including law firms, educational institutions, as well as non-profits. Whether it is an annual report or an in-house newsletter, I can come to your location and capture the images which say what you want about your business. Being able to travel to your location and still maintain a high level of lighting and composition saves time and money for your employees and your design team.

Please contact me about rates, availability, or just to talk about upcoming projects at thomas@thomasgiroir.com or 860-236-2276.
